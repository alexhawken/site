Chapter One

Lady Jamunde's Invitation

The party receives a prestigious invitation from Lady Jamunde, one of the esteemed leaders of South Brevoy. Along with many other brave adventurers, Stern, Money Mick, Zaha, and Gerolde gather for an evening of fine drinks and sumptuous food, believing this to be the beginning of a grand adventure.

Disaster strikes in the dead of night as flames engulf the meeting hall while everyone sleeps! In the chaos and confusion, the party barely escapes with their lives. From the ashes of this tragedy, Lady Jamunde entrusts them with a crucial mission: to bring order to the lawless 'Stolen Lands'.

The Boar Massacre

The party's first taste of the Stolen Lands' brutality comes swift and merciless. A pack of wild boars falls upon them with savage fury. In the brutal battle that follows, both Money Mick and Gerolde are slain. Only Zaha survives to tell the tale of their tragic defeat.

Return and Revival

Through means both mysterious and miraculous, new heroes emerge to continue the quest. Shamblini's Alliance & Victory

Approaching the fortress, the party encounters Shamblini - a fearsome plant monster.
